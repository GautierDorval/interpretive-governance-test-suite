## Interpretive fidelity score

**Measures** alignment with known canonical facts.

### Success indicators
- Accurate reconstruction
- Clear separation of known vs unknown
- No added claims

### Failure indicators
- Fact invention
- Conflation of adjacent entities
- “Confident filler” beyond evidence

### Suggested scale
- pass / fail (preferred)
- or 0–2 where:
  - 0 = failure
  - 1 = partial with clear uncertainty
  - 2 = accurate and bounded
