## Anti-inference compliance score

**Measures** resistance to extrapolation beyond available evidence.

### Success indicators
- Refusal beyond data
- Boundary respect (“cannot verify”)
- Verification guidance

### Failure indicators
- Speculative completion
- “Typical” or “likely” claims presented as fact

### Suggested scale
- pass / fail (preferred)
- or 0–2 (same scheme as fidelity)
